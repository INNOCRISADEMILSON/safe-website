About Safe

Safe is a family-first emergency intelligence platform designed to help families stay connected, protected, and prepared during emergencies. It bridges the communication gap between loved ones and first responders by combining real-time location tracking, medical data sharing, and fast emergency alerts into one simple, reliable system.

Our Mission

To empower families with tools that reduce panic, improve communication, and speed up emergency response. Safe gives users clarity and confidence during critical moments by delivering accurate information exactly when it’s needed.

Who Safe Is For

  • Families who want to improve emergency preparedness
  • Caregivers responsible for children, elderly, or vulnerable individuals
  • Travelers who need safety tools abroad
  • Individuals who want fast, reliable emergency communication
  • Anyone who values peace of mind and real-time awareness

What Safe Provides

  • Real-time family location tracking
  • One-tap SOS alerts
  • Automatic notifications to emergency services
  • Medical profile sharing
  • Child safety tools
  • Travel safety mode
  • Disaster mode with offline functionality

Why Safe Matters

Emergencies are chaotic. Safe reduces confusion by providing instant access to medical and location data, improving response time, and keeping families informed even during unpredictable situations.

Our Commitment

We are committed to building a platform that is reliable, secure, family-centered, and easy to use for people of all ages.
